Description:

Immerse yourself in the dynamic world of Marketing Management with our comprehensive course. The Marketing Management curriculum covers marketing fundamentals, market analysis, strategic marketing planning, and the essentials of digital marketing. Engage in hands-on exercises and real-world case studies, providing you with practical skills to excel in the ever-evolving field of marketing.
